About Gigi Fairview Design

Gigi Fairview Design is proud to offer furniture crafted in Juarez, Mexico. Our curated selection includes exceptional outdoor furniture, Sunbrella fabrics, umbrellas, pillows, poufs, planters, and other exquisite accessories, all designed to elevate your outdoor living spaces.

Our Services

  • Professional and ASID licensed design
  • Meticulous space planning with advanced 2D/3D software
  • Seamless white-glove installation with removal of all packing material
  • Timely delivery, with average ordering times of 6-10 weeks
  • Service where 'se habla español' for our valued clientele
